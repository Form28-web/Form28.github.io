Audio Bible Player

Pursuing the Great Commission means reaching people in ways they can receive. For over 700 million people, God’s written Word is simply not accessible. The UNESCO Institute for Statistics reports that about 13–14% of the world is fully illiterate, This does not include a large demographic with low literacy. In addition, hundreds of millions live in oral cultures or naturally receive information best through spoken word (as I do), regardless of their ability to read.

Dedicated audio Bible players provide a powerful solution, delivering distraction-free, spoken Scripture to those who need to hear it most. Our mission is to create an intuitive audio Bible device that removes technological barriers and helps people engage with Scripture naturally. By combining thoughtful user interface design with accessible hardware, we aim to build a device that allows users to navigate the audio Bible as easily as one would a physical copy.
