Audio Bible Player

Pursuing the Great Commission involves sharing the Word of God in ways people can receive and understand. For over 750 million people, God’s written Word is simply not accessible. The UNESCO Institute for Statistics reports that approximately 12% of the world's adult population is fully illiterate. This does not include the hundreds of millions more who are technically literate but lack the reading proficiency needed to meaningfully engage with a text Bible. In addition, billions of peopel live in oral cultures or naturally receive information best through spoken word, regardless of their ability to read.

Dedicated audio Bible players provide a powerful solution, delivering distraction-free, spoken Scripture to those who need to hear it most. Our mission is to create an intuitive audio Bible device that removes technological barriers and helps people engage with Scripture naturally. By combining thoughtful user interface design with accessible hardware, we aim to build a device that allows users to navigate the digital audio Bible as easily as one would a physical text copy.
