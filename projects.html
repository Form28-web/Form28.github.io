Audio Bible Player

Our mission is to create a simple, durable, and deeply intuitive audio Bible device that removes technological barriers and helps people engage with Scripture naturally—whether they are brand new to the Bible or have studied it for years. By combining thoughtful design with accessible hardware, we aim to deliver a tool that allows users to navigate the Bible as easily as they would a physical book—quickly moving between books, chapters, and passages—without the complexity of smartphones or screens that distract. This project exists to make God’s Word more approachable, more usable, and more present in everyday life, especially for those with limited access to technology, literacy challenges, or a desire for a focused, distraction-free experience.
